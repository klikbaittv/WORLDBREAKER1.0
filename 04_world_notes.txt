FORMAT
================================================================

When adding world notes, use this format:

  --- WORLD NOTE: [Topic] ---
  Date added:  [YYYY-MM-DD]
  Category:    [setting | history | culture | geography | atmosphere | rule | other]
  Content:
    [Write the world detail here. As much or as little as needed.]
  --- END NOTE ---

The AI will output this block when you say "add to world notes: [topic and details]".
You paste it below the most recent entry.

Notes can be short observations or long entries — both are valid.
The point is that the information lives here and doesn't get lost.
